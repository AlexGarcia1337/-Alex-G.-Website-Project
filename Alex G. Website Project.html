The Overall Plot

The first act is set in the late 1950's with a white family moving out of their home due to the recent incident of their sons suicide, who was driven to the brink of death because of the recent war he was drafted into. As the family moves out a black family moves in stirring up lots of tension within the neighborhood. The white residents believe if Black families move into their neighborhood they will ruin the quality of it and turn it into garbage.
